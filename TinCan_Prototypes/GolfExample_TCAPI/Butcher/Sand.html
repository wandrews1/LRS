Sanding

Sand Surface

Sand the entire surface with a random orbit palm sander. The random orbit sanders are better than using a finishing sander for this task because the random orbit doesn't leave any streaks in the surface of the wood. Start with 40 grit paper and sand until smooth. Then move up in grit. Work your way through the following grits:

  • 40
  • 80
  • 120
  • 180
  • 220
  • 320

Don't forget the back, edges, and sides

People often forget about the back, edges, and sides when sanding, but you can easily tell from holding it that proper sanding has been overlooked.
